Actor

Adrienne remained in Galway, Ireland, after having received her degree, attracted by the burgeoning performing arts in the city. Since then, she has become a mainstay of the industry. Some notable roles:

  • Girl at Bar in Gwyn Productions’ An Poitín (Promo)
  • Pussy Pillow in Eibhleann Caffrey’s What a Pussy! (Stage)
  • Gemma in Branstorm Theatre’s production of To Be Adored. (Stage)
  • Ensemble member in JokerThief’s production of You Can Be a Menace Too! (Musical)
  • Fabiana Aziza Cunningham, lawyer for the defence in UG Dramsoc’s 2019 production of The Last Days of Judas Iscariot (Stage)
  • Lily in Jailbreakers! Directed by Conor O’ Toole (Radio)
  • Lawyer for the Prosecution/ Nurse in UG Drama Department’s Machinal (Stage)
