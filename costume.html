Costume Design

Adrienne is a creative and self-motivated person, with a passion for design in all aspects of life. She is open to taking creative risks and experimenting with new mediums of designs and crafts. She holds a diploma in Graphic Design from the University College of Dublin and is currently finishing a Postgraduate Diploma in Fashion Design from the University College Dublin. Adrienne designed and crafted costumes for UG Dramsoc’s production of Rebirth. Adrienne had the opportunity to be Costume Assistant for Druid Theatre Company’s production of The Last Return which feature at the Galway International Arts Festival in 2022. She also worked in costume maintenance for Life and Times of Michael K produced by Baxter Theatre Centre as well as How to be a Dancer in Seventy-two Thousand Easy Lessons produced by Teac Damsa both of which featured at the Galway International Arts Festival in 2023.
