About Adrienne

Adrienne’s distinct talents were cultivated over a lifetime of wide-ranging experiences in a variety of locales. Adrienne was born in New Jersey, USA, where she stayed for 6 year before her family moved back to Kiltimagh, Co. Mayo, Ireland. Showing an early talent in a variety of arts, she went on to further hone her artistic skills. She holds a BA Drama, Theatre and Performance from University of Galway. She has partaken in a number of classels including a module in Voice & Diction with Deborah Martin, Berea College, Kentucky, vocal Training with Shareese Johnson, Berea College, Kentucky a Meisner Technique Workshop with James Price at the Actor’s Studio, New York, a Chekhov Technique Workshop with Max Hafler and a Devising Workshop with Moonfish Theatre.
